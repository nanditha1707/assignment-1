1. Do you offer gluten-free options?

Yes, we have a selection of gluten-free items. Please ask our staff for assistance.

2. What are your most popular pastries?

Our customers love our croissants and cinnamon rolls. They are freshly baked and available daily.

3. Can I place a custom cake order for a special occasion?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom cakes for various occasions. Contact us in advance to discuss your preferences and design ideas.

4. Do you use organic or locally sourced ingredients?

We strive to use high-quality ingredients, and whenever possible, we source locally and use organic products to create our delicious treats.

5. Are there vegan options available?

Yes, we offer a variety of vegan pastries and desserts. Check our menu or ask our staff for the current vegan selections.

6. Do you provide catering services for events?

Yes, we offer catering services for events of all sizes. Contact us for more information and to discuss your catering needs.

7. What is the shelf life of your products?

Our products are made fresh daily, and most are best enjoyed within a day or two. For specific shelf life information, please ask our staff.

8. Can I pre-order pastries for pickup?

Certainly! Pre-orders are welcomed for your convenience. Contact us or visit our website to place an order.

9. Do you have a loyalty program or rewards program?

Yes, we offer a loyalty program for our valued customers. Earn points with each purchase and enjoy exclusive rewards.

10. Are gift cards available?

Yes, we offer gift cards in various denominations. They make perfect gifts for friends and family who appreciate delicious baked goods.

11. Do you host baking classes or workshops?

Yes, we occasionally host baking classes and workshops. Follow our social media or check our website for upcoming events.

12. What precautions are you taking for food allergies?

We take food allergies seriously. Please inform our staff about any allergies, and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.
